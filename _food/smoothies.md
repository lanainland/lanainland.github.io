**Serves 1**

## Ingredients

### Berry smoothie

- 1 Banana
- 1/4 cup frozen berries (raspberries, blueberries or strawberries)
- 1 cup or 250 ml almond milk

### Banana and oats smoothie

- 1 Banana
- 1/4 cup ice
- 1/4 cup oats
- 1 cup or 250 ml almond milk
- 1 tsp honey
- 1/4 tsp cinnamon

## Instructions

1. Blend all ingredients until smooth.
1. Serve in a cold glass.
